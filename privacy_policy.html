Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

1. Introduction

OruShops ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and safeguarding of your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and authentication credentials.
  • Shop Information: Store name, location, and business details you enter during setup.
  • Inventory Data: Product details, stock quantities, batch information, pricing, and sales records you create and manage.
  • Customer Data: Khata (credit) records and customer information you enter for your business operations.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Camera Access: Camera permission is requested for QR/barcode scanning only. Images and scan data are not stored or transmitted.
  • Photos: Photo library access is requested for product image uploads. We do not store metadata or access logs.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version (for analytics and crash reporting).
  • Crash Data: Error logs and crash information are collected only with your explicit consent for app improvement.

2.3 Information NOT Collected

  • Biometric data is never stored or transmitted (used only for local authentication)
  • Payment information is never stored (processed by RevenueCat)
  • Location data is not collected
  • Device contacts are not accessed

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (inventory management, sales tracking, etc.)
  • To process authentication and secure your account
  • To enable cloud sync and backup of your data (with your consent)
  • To improve the App through anonymized analytics and crash reporting (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights

4. Data Security and Storage

  • Local Storage: Most data (products, inventory, sales) is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database.
  • Cloud Storage: Account authentication data is stored securely on Firebase.
  • Encryption: Data in transit uses HTTPS encryption. Local database is protected by device-level security.
  • Access Control: Only you can access your data through your authenticated account.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your information. We may share data with trusted third parties only as necessary:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and backup services
  • RevenueCat: For subscription and in-app purchase processing
  • Analytics Providers: Anonymized usage data only (with your consent)
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • You can view, edit, and delete your data anytime within the App
  • You can disable analytics and crash reporting in Settings > Privacy
  • You can deny camera, photo, and biometric permissions at any time

6.2 Data Deletion

You can request complete deletion of all your data from our servers by navigating to Settings > Account > Request Data Deletion. We will process deletion requests within 30 days and confirm completion via email.

6.3 GDPR, CCPA, and Other Regulations

  • GDPR (EU Users): You have rights to access, rectification, erasure, and portability of your data.
  • CCPA (California Users): You have rights to know, delete, and opt-out of sale of your data (we do not sell).
  • LGPD (Brazil Users): Similar rights to data access, correction, and deletion.

7. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies or web beacons. We do not engage in cross-app tracking or behavioral profiling.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Retention of Data

  • Local Data: Retained on your device indefinitely until you delete or reset
  • Account Data: Retained until you request deletion or account closure
  • Crash/Analytics Data: Retained for up to 30 days for diagnostic purposes
  • Backup Data: Retained according to your cloud backup settings and provider terms

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
